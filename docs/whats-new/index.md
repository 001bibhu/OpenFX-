# What's new

Product updates and platform improvements from OpenFX.

---

## June 2026

### New corridors and currency coverage

- **PHP (Philippine Peso) launched**, new payout corridor with local rails; available now
- **COP (Colombian Peso) and ARS (Argentine Peso)**, in beta; expanding emerging-market access
- **AED (UAE Dirham)**, active corridor with local payout support
- Platform now supports **40+ currency pairs** and **25+ local payment rails**

### Platform and treasury

- **Automated treasury workflows**, deposit → trade → withdraw sequences with full visibility in a single dashboard
- **Real-time money tracking**, live status on deposits, withdrawals, and settlements, including after-hours requests completed in under 60 minutes
- **Multi-currency account management**, hold and manage USD, EUR, GBP, and corridor currencies from one interface
- **Enterprise role management**, granular permissions to assign roles and control team access

### Trading API

- **REST, FIX, and streaming quote support** for programmatic trading workflows
- Time-lock guarantee on quoted rates for automated quote → trade flows
- Webhook events for `trade.settled` and `withdrawal.completed`

## May 2026

### Settlement performance

- **90% of trades settle in under 60 minutes**; 30% in under 10 minutes
- **24/7/365 operations**, move money on weekends, holidays, and after banking hours
- After-hours withdrawals processed in under 60 minutes

### Liquidity and scale

- **$70B+ total payment volume** processed on the platform, driven by automated treasury adoption among institutional clients
- Consistent pricing at volume, whether moving $5M or $50M, spreads stay predictable

## March 2026

### Payment rails

- **SEPA Instant** for EUR, near-instant settlement via local partner banks
- **Faster Payments (FPS)** for GBP
- **NPP (New Payments Platform)** for AUD, real-time settlement using local account number and BSB
- **USDC and USDT** stablecoin support for on-ramping, off-ramping, and cross-border transfers

### Onboarding

- Institutional onboarding reduced to **under 72 hours** (vs. six months or more with traditional FX providers)
- Personalized **sandbox environment** for testing quotes, trades, and withdrawals before production
