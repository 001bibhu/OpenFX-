## General & onboarding

### What is OpenFX?

OpenFX is a next-generation cross-border payment infrastructure provider. We enable institutions to move money globally with near-instant settlement, deep liquidity, and exceptional cost efficiency. Our infrastructure combines the reliability of traditional finance with the speed and programmability of modern blockchain rails.

### What are the key benefits of using OpenFX?

The main benefits include:

- **Eliminating pre-funding requirements** — access liquidity on demand without nostro accounts in every corridor
- **Superior speed** — 90% of trades settle in under 60 minutes
- **Consistent pricing at any volume** — transparent spreads with no hidden markups
- **Global reach with local compliance** — 40+ currency pairs and 25+ local rails
- **Treasury optimization** — multi-currency balances, real-time tracking, and internal fund movements

### Who can use OpenFX?

We work exclusively with **institutional clients**: remittance companies, fintechs, payment processors, digital banks, brokers, and investment platforms. If you require real-time settlement across fiat and stablecoin rails, OpenFX is purpose-built for your needs.

### What types of clients do you serve?

OpenFX caters to enterprise-level clients: remittance companies, neobanks, brokerages, payment processors, and payroll providers across a wide range of industries and geographies.

### Can I use OpenFX for personal transactions?

No. We are designed for businesses and enterprises. Our current focus is on serving institutional and corporate clients.

### How do I get started?

Schedule a demo at [openfx.com](https://www.openfx.com/), complete KYC, and our team will guide you through onboarding — typically within **72 hours**.

### What is the onboarding process like?

Our onboarding is straightforward: sign up, complete KYC and compliance verification, receive sandbox access, and begin testing or trading. Most clients go live in under 72 hours — compared to six months or more with traditional FX providers.

### How do you compare to other FX providers?

OpenFX stands out for laser focus on speed, cost-efficiency, and transparency. We do not offer services beyond core FX and money movement, allowing us to maintain a highly specialized platform. Our liquidity management — leveraging stablecoins and dynamic balance sheet allocation — delivers unparalleled pricing and settlement times compared to traditional banks.

### How do I scale my usage as my business grows?

Our platform handles high-volume transactions without disruption. As your volume grows, we scale infrastructure, liquidity, and support while offering competitive, consistent pricing through volume-based tiers.

## Product & features

### Do you support stablecoin transactions?

Yes. We support **USDC and USDT** for on-ramping, off-ramping, and cross-border transfers. Crypto deposits settle in near real-time, subject to blockchain speed and congestion.

### How fast are trades settled?

90% of trades settle in **under 60 minutes**; 30% in under 10 minutes. This is significantly faster than the 2–7 day timelines typical of traditional FX providers.

### What is the user experience?

We offer T+0 (same-day) settlement for many transactions. You can execute trades via a **web-based GUI** or **Trading API** (REST, FIX, and streaming quotes) — whichever fits your workflow.

### What currencies and payment rails do you support?

We support major G20 currencies and many emerging markets, with banking infrastructure in the US, UK, Europe, UAE, Mexico, Brazil, and Southeast Asia. The platform covers **40+ currency pairs** and **25+ local rails**.

Recent corridor expansion includes **PHP** (launched), **COP** and **ARS** (beta), and **AED** (active).

### Do you support SEPA Instant and Faster Payments (FPS)?

Yes. We support **SEPA Instant** for EUR and **FPS** for GBP through local partner banks. Settlement is typically near-instant, though some counterparties may impose business-hour limitations.

### Do you support the Australian NPP system?

Yes. We support AUD via **NPP (New Payments Platform)** for real-time settlement using local account number and BSB code.

### Can I integrate your API into my platform?

Absolutely. We have robust REST APIs for quotes, trades, deposits, and withdrawals, plus webhooks for real-time settlement events. See [API overview](../platform/api-overview.md).

### Can trades be canceled once confirmed?

Once confirmed, trades cannot be canceled. You may reverse the position by executing a new trade in the opposite direction. In some instances, OpenFX may offer a one-time spread credit, subject to approval.

### Do you support chat-based trading (Telegram or Slack)?

No. All trades must be executed via our secure web GUI or Trading API.

### How quickly will my deposit reflect?

Fiat deposits typically reflect within **20 minutes** once received. Crypto deposits settle in near real-time. Timelines may vary depending on the sending institution and rail used.

### Can you integrate with our treasury management systems?

Yes. We offer APIs, webhooks, and a web-based platform. We work with your team to develop a tailored integration plan so OpenFX becomes an embedded part of your treasury infrastructure.

### Can you support treasury management and internal fund movements?

Absolutely. Hold multi-currency balances, execute FX conversions, withdraw to preferred bank accounts, and move funds between currency accounts — all with real-time settlement.

### Do you offer reporting or analytics?

Yes. We provide detailed reporting and real-time analytics on transactions, deposits, and settlements to inform treasury and compliance decisions.

### Do you offer named accounts for clients?

Not currently. Virtual account solutions are on the roadmap.

### Can I send RTP to my OpenFX USD account?

USD does not currently support RTP. The fastest option is intrabank transfer via our partner bank (Cross River Bank) in the United States.

### What is your product roadmap?

We are focused on adding emerging market currencies, virtual account solutions, and enhanced treasury management tools. See [What's new](../whats-new/) for recent releases.

## Pricing & fees

### What is your pricing model?

Pricing is based on the mid-market rate plus a small spread: **3–5 bps for G20**, **10–12 bps for emerging markets**. No additional fees or markups. Traditional banks often charge 20–50+ bps for expedited settlement.

### How do volume-based pricing tiers work?

The more you transact, the better rates you receive. Clients doing **$30–500M monthly** can expect spreads in the 3–5 bps range. Clients above **$100M monthly** may qualify for additional pricing benefits.

### Do you have minimum volume requirements?

No strict minimums, but most clients benefit from volume-based pricing tiers.

### Can I try the platform before committing?

Yes. We offer a **sandbox environment** to test the platform, execute sample trades, and see pricing in real-time.

## Security & compliance

### How secure is your platform?

Security is a top priority. We use industry-leading protocols, encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular penetration testing. Infrastructure is built with redundancy and failover for 24/7 availability.

### How do you handle AML/KYC?

We partner with licensed and regulated entities in each market. We perform due diligence on senders and receivers and integrate with clients' existing compliance workflows.

### How do you maintain liquidity?

We maintain a dynamic balance sheet allocated across currencies and markets. Our market-making engine continuously hedges exposures using banks, FX brokers, and stablecoin OTC desks.

### How do you handle market volatility?

We maintain a diversified balance sheet and liquidity provider network. Spreads may widen temporarily during acute liquidity constraints, with transparency provided at quote time.
